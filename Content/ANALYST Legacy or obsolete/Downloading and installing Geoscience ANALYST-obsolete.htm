Downloading and installing Geoscience ANALYST

Geoscience ANALYST is a free product available for download from the website.

To download and install Geoscience ANALYST:

  1. Follow this link.
  2. Once you are on the Geoscience ANALYST web page, click Download Now.

 


  1. From the Geoscience ANALYST download portal, check the box next to Geoscience ANALYST v _setup.exe (i) then click Download Selected (ii)

.

 

  1. Open the downloaded file and in the Geoscience ANALYST setup wizard, click I accept the agreement (iii), then click Next (iv).

 

  1. Click Next again, or if you prefer, choose a different folder in which to install Geoscience ANALYST.
  1. Click Next again, then click Install (v).


  2. Click Finish (vi).
