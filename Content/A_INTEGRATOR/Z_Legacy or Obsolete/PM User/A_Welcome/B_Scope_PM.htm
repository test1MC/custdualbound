Scope: Project management

This guide covers the functionality of Geoscience INTEGRATOR and includes examples of practical applications you are likely to encounter as a user who is managing a project and who will need to know the software thoroughly. The case studies contained throughout the guide aim to demonstrate how Geoscience INTEGRATOR excels as a user-friendly yet robust tool capable of complex data management tasks.

You may use Geoscience INTEGRATOR to import, integrate, organize, and manipulate geoscientific project data. To accurately assess the gathered data, projects contain a multitude of specifications and monitoring options, such as:

  • 3D search spaces
  • Coordinate system
  • Reports
  • Shifts
  • Units
  • Data files and sets
  • Classifications
  • Tags

You may use Geoscience INTEGRATOR in conjunction with Geoscience ANALYST to provide front-end 3D graphics based on the data compiled in a project, through a dedicated link that allows advanced querying options.
