Launching Geoscience INTEGRATOR

To launch Geoscience INTEGRATOR and change your password:

  1. Enter the URL integrator.yourdomain.com (Actual URL is client specific)
  2. Enter your username or email and temporary password in the fields provided.
  3. Click Login.
  4. On the sidebar, click Main and then Edit Profile.
  5. In the Change your password dialog, enter your new password and confirm it, and click Update.

 

If you:

Role Instruction
Are a system administrator: Mira Geoscience has set up your account and provided you with a username and password.
Are given access by a system administrator: Check your email for a link to Geoscience INTEGRATOR and a username and password
Forgot your password: Click Forgot your Password? and follow the instructions to reset it in the auto-generated email sent to your account.
Do not know your username: Contact Mira Geoscience support directly.
