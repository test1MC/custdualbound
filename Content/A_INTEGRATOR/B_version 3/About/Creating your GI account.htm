Creating your Geoscience INTEGRATOR account

Once a system administrator creates your account, monitor your email for a link to , and follow the instructions below.

To set your Geoscience INTEGRATOR account password:

  1. Follow the link provided in the body of the email you received from integrator@mirageoscience.com.
  2. Provide your email address and set your password.
  3. Click Login.

The link will expire after seven days, after which you can request a new one by clicking Forgot your password? on the login page.

Additionally, you can change your password at any time by accessing the Edit profile menu at the top right of the  user interface.
