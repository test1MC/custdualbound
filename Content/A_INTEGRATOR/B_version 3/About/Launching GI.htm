Launching

Once a system administrator creates your account, monitor your email for a link to along with a user name and password, and follow the instructions below:

To launch Geoscience INTEGRATOR and change your password:

  1. In the body of the email you received from integrator@mirageoscience.com, click the URL provided.
  2. Create your account by entering your email and password in the fields provided.
  3. Click Login.
  4. On the sidebar, click Main and then Edit Profile.
  5. In the Change your password dialogue box, enter your new password and confirm it, and click Update.
The download link will expire after seven days, after which you will have to be sent another one.
